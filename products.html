For MFIs

CohusDex Score

Credit intelligence for microfinance institutions. Lend with confidence to the 500 million workers traditional banks ignore.

Behavioral credit scoring Daily cash flow analysis Default risk prediction Group lending profiles Portfolio risk dashboard Repayment pattern insights

Traditional credit scores don't work for the informal sector. No payslips. No bank statements. No collateral. Score builds credit profiles from what informal workers actually have: daily transaction history, business patterns, and community reputation — captured naturally through Faidaza.

For FMCGs

CohusDex Pulse

Market intelligence for FMCG companies. Know what 500 million informal consumers actually buy, when they buy it, and at what price point.

Real-time purchase tracking Price sensitivity mapping Product affinity analysis Regional demand patterns Stock movement velocity Competitor share estimation

FMCG companies spend millions on market research that never reaches the informal sector. Pulse captures purchase data directly from traders — not surveys, not panels, not guesses. Actual buying behavior from the 85% of African retail that happens outside formal channels.

For Governments & Development Agencies

CohusDex Atlas

Economic mapping for governments and development agencies. See the invisible economy — in real time, at street level.

Informal sector GDP estimation Trade corridor mapping Employment pattern analysis Poverty indicator tracking Policy impact simulation Cross-border trade insights

Governments make economic policy for the informal sector blindfolded — because they can't measure what they can't see. Atlas turns daily trader transactions into economic maps: where money flows, what sectors are growing, and which communities need support.
