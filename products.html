CohusDex Score

Powered by advanced statistical models trained on informal sector transaction data.

CohusDex Pulse

For FMCG & Distribution Companies

Real-time market intelligence from Africa's informal economy. Understand pricing dynamics, demand patterns, and distribution flows at the street level. Know what's selling, where, and at what price — without sending survey teams. Optimize your supply chain with ground-truth data.

Aggregated, anonymized market intelligence from thousands of touchpoints.

CohusDex Atlas

For Governments & Development Agencies

The first real-time map of the informal economy. Visualize economic activity at the neighborhood level. Track shifts in employment, income, and market health. Make policy decisions based on current data — not a census from 5 years ago. Finally see the economy that's always been invisible.
