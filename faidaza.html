Kwa Nini Faidaza?

Imejengwa kwa uhalisia wa Afrika. Sio kwa Silicon Valley.

  • 🎤

    Voice-First — Sema Tu

    Unazungumza tu. Faidaza inakusikia na kukujibu kwa Kiswahili. Hakuna kuchapa — sema tu "nimeuza nyanya zote" nayo itafanya hesabu. Just speak. Faidaza listens and responds in Kiswahili. No typing needed.

  • 📡

    100% Offline — Hakuna Internet

    Inafanya kazi bila internet. Soko halina WiFi? Hakuna shida. Faidaza inaishi kwenye simu yako. Data yako inabaki kwako. Works completely offline. No internet needed. Your data stays on your phone.

  • 📱

    Simu ya KES 3,000 Inatosha

    Haihitaji simu ya bei ghali. Inafanya kazi kwenye Android ya kawaida — hata ile ya KES 3,000. Chini ya 30MB. Runs on a basic Android phone. Under 30MB. No expensive device needed.

  • 🗣️

    Kiswahili na Lugha za Kienyeji

    Inazungumza Kiswahili — lugha yako. Lugha zaidi za kienyeji zinakuja. Sio English-App-For-Africa. Speaks Kiswahili and more local languages coming. Built in Africa, for Africa.

  • 💰

    Bure Milele — Hakuna Malipo

    Faidaza ni bure kwa wafanyakazi wote. Hakuna ada. Hakuna usajili wa kulipa. CFO wako wa bure, daima. Free forever for all workers. No fees. No premium tier. Your free CFO, always.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓

Je, Faidaza ni bure kweli? Is Faidaza really free?

Ndiyo. Bure kabisa kwa wafanyakazi wote. Hakuna ada iliyofichwa. Biashara yetu inapata mapato kutoka kwa taasisi (MFIs, FMCGs) wanaonunua data isiyotambulisha mtu binafsi — sio kutoka kwako. Yes. Completely free. No hidden fees. We make money from institutions buying anonymized insights — not from you.

❓

Data yangu inakaa wapi? Where is my data stored?

Data yako yote inakaa kwenye simu yako. Hatuna ufikiaji wake. Hatuuzi data yako. Unapounganisha kwenye internet (hiari), data isiyotambulisha inaweza kuchangia kuboresha bidhaa na utafiti. All your data stays on your phone. We don't access it. We don't sell it. Optional internet sync contributes anonymized data to improve products.

❓

Inafanya kazi kwenye simu gani? What phones does it work on?

Android 5.0 na kuendelea. Inafanya kazi kwenye simu za bei ya chini (kama Tecno, Itel, Samsung ya kawaida). Haina haja ya RAM kubwa. Android 5.0+. Works on budget phones. No high RAM needed.

❓

Lugha gani zinapatikana? What languages are available?

Kiswahili kwa sasa. Lugha zaidi zinakuja: Dholuo, Kikuyu, Kamba, Luhya, na nyinginezo. Kiswahili now. More coming: Dholuo, Kikuyu, Kamba, Luhya, and others.
