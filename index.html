The Problem

M-Pesa Shows Transactions.
Faidaza Shows Business.

500 million informal workers in Africa. Most use mobile money. None of them get useful business data from it. This is the gap we close.

⚠️ What M-Pesa Tells You

A Transaction Statement

Balance brought forward KES 300
M-Pesa received KES 200
M-Pesa sent -KES 400
Net -KES 100

This tells you nothing about your business. You could be profitable or losing money — M-Pesa can't tell the difference.

VS
✅ What Faidaza Tells You

A Business Record

Bought 5kg tomatoes -KES 300
Sold 3kg @ KES 100/kg KES 300
2kg remaining (inventory) KES 200 value
Profit (3kg sold) KES 120

Now you know: you made KES 120 profit on the 3kg sold, and you still have 2kg worth KES 200 in stock. This is business intelligence.

FAQ

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara

Faidaza ni bure kweli?

Ndiyo. Bure kabisa. Siku zote. Hakuna usajili, hakuna matangazo, hakuna malipo ya kufichwa. Faidaza itakuwa bure milele kwa wafanyakazi wa kujiajiri. Mapato ya CohusDex yanatoka kwa taasisi — microfinance, FMCG, serikali, na bima — ambao wananunua data na intelligence (kwa idhini ya mtumiaji). Sio kutoka kwa mama mboga.

Inafanya kazi bila internet?

Ndiyo. AI yote — speech-to-text, language model, na business analytics — inafanya kazi moja kwa moja kwenye simu yako. Hakuna data inayotoka nje bila ruhusa yako. Utahitaji internet mara ya kwanza tu kupakua modeli za AI (~370MB). Baada ya hapo, Faidaza inafanya kazi offline.

Lugha gani zinapatikana?

Kiswahili, Luo, na Kikuyu sasa. Tunaongeza: Luganda, Kinyarwanda, Kamba, Kalenjin, Luhya, Dholuo, na lugha 20+ zaidi kadri tunavyosonga Afrika nzima. Kila mwezi lugha mpya inaongezwa.

Inahitaji simu gani?

Android 8.0 au zaidi, RAM 2GB. Inafanya kazi kwenye simu za KES 3,000. Faidaza APK (~30MB) ndio inapakuliwa kwanza; modeli za AI (~370MB) zinapakuliwa mara ya kwanza unapofungua app. Baada ya hapo, inafanya kazi offline kabisa. Nafasi yote inayohitajika: ~400MB.

Data yangu iko salama?

Ndiyo kabisa. Database yako imesimbwa kwa SQLCipher (AES-256). Data yako haiondoki simu yako bila ruhusa yako. Sync ni optional, opt-in, na data inayotumwa inafanywa anonymous — hatuwezi kukutambulisha wewe binafsi. Faragha yako ni msingi wa biashara yetu.
