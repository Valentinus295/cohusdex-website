Who We Are

CohusDex was founded by Valentine Owuor, an Economics & Statistics student at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology in Kenya. His mother is a vegetable seller in Migori Market. In twenty years, nobody ever gave her a tool to know her daily profit.

"My mother sells vegetables every day. She is one of the hardest-working people I know. But in twenty years, she has never tracked one transaction. She doesn't know her profit. Not because she's lazy. Because nobody ever built a tool for her."

Valentine is building CohusDex to change that — for his mother, and for 500 million informal workers across Africa who earn a living every day but don't know the numbers behind their livelihood.

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara

Faidaza ni bure?

Ndiyo. Bure kabisa. Siku zote. Hakuna usajili, matangazo, wala malipo ya kufichwa. Tunalipwa na makampuni yanayotaka data ya soko — sio na wafanyakazi.

Inafanya kazi bila internet?

Ndiyo. AI yote inafanya kazi kwenye simu yako. Hakuna data inayotoka nje bila ruhusa yako.

Nani anaweza kutumia Faidaza?

Kila mfanyakazi wa kujiajiri. Mama mboga, boda-boda, fundi, mvuvi, mkulima, mama lishe, mama fua, kinyozi, mjenzi, dereva, mitumba, M-Pesa agent, na wengine 36+. Sekta 8 tayari. Zaidi inakuja kadri tunavyosonga Afrika nzima.

Lugha gani?

Kiswahili, Luo, na Kikuyu sasa. Tunaongeza lugha zaidi kila mwezi — Luganda, Kinyarwanda, Amharic, Hausa, na 20+ zaidi kadri tunavyosonga Afrika nzima.

Mnatofautiana vipi na M-Pesa?

M-Pesa inasonga pesa. Faidaza inaelewa pesa. M-Pesa inajua KES 300 ilitoka. Faidaza inajua ulinunua nyanya kilo 5 kwa KES 60, ukauza kilo 3 kwa KES 100, faida yako ni KES 120. M-Pesa ni mkoba. Faidaza ni CFO.

Nani aliunda CohusDex?

Valentine Owuor, mwanafunzi wa Economics & Statistics Masinde Muliro University, Migori, Kenya. Alijenga Faidaza kwa sababu mamake ni mama mboga na aliona hakuna mtu aliyewahi kumpa chombo cha kuelewa hesabu zake — si Kenya, si Afrika, si dunia.
