What is BFS?

Beta-Financial Score (BFS) is a credit scoring model designed specifically for informal workers who have no traditional credit history.

5 Dimensions

  • Revenue Stability
  • Margin Discipline
  • Inventory Turnover
  • Chama Participation
  • Household-Business Separation

For MFIs

API access: GET /bfs/score/{trader_id}

Returns: BFS score (0-1000), confidence interval, risk category
